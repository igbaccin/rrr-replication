- With the removal of farmer subsidies in major grain-producing countries, international staple food prices rise, driving dependence on non-agricultural economic activities (Bryceson_2002 p.11)

- The extraction cost of coal was influenced by the pithead price and rents paid to landowners (Clark&Jacks_2007 p.14)
