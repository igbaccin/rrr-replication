- By the elaboration of the mixed economy being a function of economic growth itself

- By hindering investment and economic progress due to certain institutions (AcemogluEtAl_2001 p.27)
- By exploiting differences in institutions as a source of exogenous variation to estimate their impact on economic performance (AcemogluEtAl_2001 p.27)
